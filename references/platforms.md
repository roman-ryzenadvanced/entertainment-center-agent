## Platform Quality Ratings

### Reliability Scale
- **5/5** — Consistently available, large catalog, well-maintained
- **4/5** — Generally reliable, good catalog
- **3/5** — Useful but with limitations (regional, smaller catalog)
- **2/5** — Limited use cases, occasional issues
- **1/5** — Unreliable or very limited

### Notes
- Regional availability varies significantly. Platforms marked with regional notes may not be available in all countries.
- "Ad-supported" means the content is free but includes commercial advertisements.
- "Free tier" means there is a premium paid option alongside the free content.
- All platforms listed have been verified as legitimate and legal services as of 2026.
